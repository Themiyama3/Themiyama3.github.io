About
Regarding things that helped develop the site

This website is dedicated to the exploration of artificial intelligence (AI). It was created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with Bootstrap, Typed.js, and AOS also being used for some features. Google Font has been used for the fonts.

The goal of this website is to provide information about AI in a way that is both informative and engaging. The website covers a wide range of topics, including the history of AI, the different types of AI, and the many applications of AI in the real world.

The website is also designed to be interactive, with features such as interactive quizzes and animated illustrations. This helps to make the learning process more fun and engaging.

I hope you enjoy exploring this website and learning more about AI!
